Sales Tracking

Tracking of sale orders according to sale person and triggering mail for monthly sale report.

Step 1:

Click on view sale button which appears on the sales team.

Step 2:

Once clicked on view sale button the sale orders done by each sale person is grouped and viewed.

Step 3:

In scheduled action an automated action is created to trigger mail for monthly sale orders done by each sale person

Step 4:

Now you can see the mail triggered by automated action.
