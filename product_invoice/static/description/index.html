Product Invoicing

In Odoo Once after the confirmation of purchase order and sale order, system will not display the Total Invoice amount for the particular product and Invoice lines. After installing the Product Invoicing Module after the sale or purchase Invoice of a product, a Smart button will appear on the screen as customer invoice and supplier invoice. When user clicks on this Smart button system will display the paid invoice lines of a product.
