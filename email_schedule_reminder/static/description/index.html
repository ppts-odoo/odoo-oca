Month End Email Scheduler

This module will trigger emails based on the selected templates at every month end, it supports both community and enterprise edition.

Step 1:

After installing the module, Month end Email scheduler icon will be displayed on the main screen.

Step 2:

Open the main menu, add the name and E-mail Template, system will trigger emails automatically based on the selected templates every last day of the month.
