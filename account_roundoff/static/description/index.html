Round Off value

In odoo default the amount total is not rounded off. This module helps to Round off total in purchase, sale and invoice.

The default amount total values are shown below.

Sale

After installing the module. In sale the total amount is rounded off and the rounded value is given.

Purchase

In purchase the total amount is rounded off and the rounded value is given. If the 'Apply round off' is not checked, the total will not be rounded off.

Accounting

Step 1:

In settings, round off should be selected.

Step 2:

In invoice the total amount is rounded off and the rounded value is given.

Step 3:

A journal item will be created for the rounded value.
